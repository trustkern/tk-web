4. Google Fonts

This website uses web fonts provided by Google LLC ("Google Fonts"). When you access a page, your browser loads the required fonts from Google's servers. This transmits your IP address to Google.

Google Fonts is used on the basis of Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR — our legitimate interest in a consistent and visually appealing presentation of our website.

For more information see Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Cookies and consent management (Klaro)

This website uses Klaro, an open-source consent management tool, to obtain and manage your consent for optional cookies and third-party services.

When you first visit the site, Klaro presents a consent dialog. Your choices are stored in a cookie called klaro in your browser. This cookie is strictly necessary and does not require consent (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR).

You can change your cookie preferences at any time by clicking "Cookie Settings" in the footer.

Strictly necessary cookies

  • klaro — stores your cookie consent preferences. Duration: 365 days. No personal data is transmitted to third parties.

Optional / analytics cookies

The following services are loaded only after you give explicit consent via the Klaro dialog:

  • Google Fonts — external font loading (see section 4).

We do not currently use analytics, advertising, or social-media tracking on this website. If this changes in the future, consent will be obtained before any such service is activated.

6. Contact by e-mail

If you contact us by e-mail (e.g. via info@trustkern.de), we process the personal data you provide (name, e-mail address, message content) for the purpose of handling your inquiry.

Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR — processing necessary for pre-contractual measures, or Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR — our legitimate interest in responding to inquiries.

Your data is deleted once your inquiry has been conclusively processed, unless statutory retention obligations apply.

7. Your rights

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) you have the following rights:

  • Right of access (Art. 15 GDPR) — request information about your stored personal data.
  • Right to rectification (Art. 16 GDPR) — request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Right to erasure (Art. 17 GDPR) — request deletion of your data, subject to legal retention requirements.
  • Right to restriction of processing (Art. 18 GDPR).
  • Right to data portability (Art. 20 GDPR).
  • Right to object (Art. 21 GDPR) — object to processing based on legitimate interest.
  • Right to withdraw consent (Art. 7(3) GDPR) — withdraw consent at any time without affecting the lawfulness of processing before withdrawal.
  • Right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority (Art. 77 GDPR). The competent authority for Berlin is: Berliner Beauftragte für Datenschutz und Informationsfreiheit, Friedrichstr. 219, 10969 Berlin.

8. Data security

This website is served over HTTPS (TLS encryption) to protect data in transit between your browser and the server.

9. Changes to this policy
